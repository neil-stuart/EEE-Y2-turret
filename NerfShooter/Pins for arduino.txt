D0: Din of XBee
D1: Dout of XBee
D2: Flywheel control
D3 & D4: Back left wheel - 3rd pins
D7 & D8: Front left wheel - 2nd pins
D9: Pan Servo
D10: Tilt Servo
D11: Fire Servo

A0 & A1: Front right wheel - 1st pins
A2 & A3: Back right wheel - 4th pins

A4 & A5: Keep free potentially for RasPi
A6 & A7: Input only, maybe sensors in future

All H - Bridge notation is from left to right 
with front of car being side closest to H - Bridges
